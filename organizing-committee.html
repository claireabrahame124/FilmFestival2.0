The government of Puducherry vide G.O. MS. No. 2 dated 05.11.2025 has reconstituted the Film Selection Committee to Select films for screenings and awards and to approve the structure and program of the Puducherry Film Festival. The Committee is headed by the Hon'ble Chief Minister and Minister ( Information & Publicity) and consists of the following members :
