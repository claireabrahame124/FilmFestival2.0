# Puducherry Film Festival Website

A complete replica of the Puducherry Film Festival website (https://puducherryfilmfest.in/)

## Design Specifications

### Colors
- **Primary Color:** #4487F2 (Blue)
- **Secondary Color:** #F6BD63 (Gold/Yellow)

### Typography
All text uses the **Myriad Pro** font family:
- **Headings:** Myriad Pro Bold
- **Subtext:** Myriad Pro Regular
- **Main Content:** Myriad Pro Regular/Italics
- **Other Information:** Myriad Pro Regular

## Website Structure

### Main Pages

1. **index.html** - Homepage
   - Hero section with festival dates and registration
   - Festival information
   - Quick links section
   - Registration popup modal

2. **about.html** - About PFF
   - Festival overview
   - Vision and mission
   - Festival highlights

3. **organizing-committee.html** - Organising Team
   - Festival leadership
   - Organizing team members
   - Advisory board

4. **awards.html** - Sankardoss Swamigal Award
   - Award information
   - Categories
   - Selection criteria

### Program Pages

5. **indian-panorama.html** - Indian Panorama section
6. **international-films.html** - International Films section
7. **childrens-films.html** - Children's Films section
8. **documentary-films.html** - Documentary Films section

### Session Pages

9. **master-classes.html** - Master Classes information

### Festival Pages

10. **gallery.html** - Photo and video gallery
11. **venue.html** - Festival venues with details

### Download Pages

12. **film-schedule.html** - Film screening schedule

### Other Pages

13. **filmmakers-paradise.html** - Puducherry as a filming location
14. **arts-crafts.html** - Arts and crafts of Puducherry
15. **padma-awardees.html** - Our Padma Awardees

### Contact

16. **contact.html** - Contact information and details

## Features

- **Responsive Navigation:** Dropdown menus for all sections
- **Mobile-Friendly:** Responsive design adapts to all screen sizes
- **Registration System:** Interactive popup for festival registration
- **Consistent Design:** All pages share unified color scheme and typography
- **Social Media Integration:** Links to Facebook and Instagram
- **External CSS:** Shared styles.css file for easy maintenance

## Usage

Simply open `index.html` in a web browser to view the website. All pages are interlinked and navigation works seamlessly across all sections.

## Key Features of Each Page

### Homepage (index.html)
- Animated hero section with festival dates
- Registration call-to-action with popup modal
- Quick links to important sections
- Full navigation menu

### About (about.html)
- Comprehensive festival information
- Vision and mission statements
- Highlights presented in grid layout

### Organizing Committee (organizing-committee.html)
- Team structure in grid format
- Leadership and support teams

### Awards (awards.html)
- Award categories with descriptions
- Selection criteria
- Award significance

### Gallery (gallery.html)
- Placeholder grid for festival photos
- Designed for easy image addition

### Venue (venue.html)
- Detailed information about all three venues:
  - Kamarajar Manimandapam
  - Alliance Française
  - Dr Abdul Kalam Puducherry Science Centre & Planetarium

### Contact (contact.html)
- Contact information cards
- Email and phone details
- Social media links
- Visitor information

### Filmmaker's Paradise (filmmakers-paradise.html)
- Filming locations in Puducherry
- Why choose Puducherry
- Notable films shot in the region
